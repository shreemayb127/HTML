Maharajas Palace Menu

Starters:

  • Samosa Platter: Crispy pastries filled with spiced potatoes and peas, served with tamarind and mint chutneys.

  • Tandoori Prawns: Jumbo prawns marinated in yogurt and spices, grilled to perfection in the tandoor.

  • Paneer Tikka: Cubes of cottage cheese marinated in a blend of spices and grilled, served with mint chutney.

  • Chicken Seekh Kebabs: Minced chicken mixed with spices, skewered and grilled in the tandoor.

Main Course:

Vegetarian:

  • Paneer Butter Masala: Cottage cheese cubes in a rich and creamy tomato-based sauce.

  • Chana Masala: Chickpeas cooked in a flavorful onion and tomato gravy with spices.

  • Baingan Bharta: Smoked and mashed eggplant cooked with tomatoes, onions, and spices.

  • Aloo Gobi: Potatoes and cauliflower cooked with turmeric, cumin, and coriander.

Non-Vegetarian:

  • Butter Chicken: Tender chicken pieces cooked in a creamy tomato sauce with a blend of spices.

  • Lamb Rogan Josh: Succulent pieces of lamb cooked in a rich, aromatic curry with Kashmiri spices.

  • Goan Fish Curry: Fish fillets simmered in a coconut milk-based curry with Goan spices.

  • Mutton Biryani: Fragrant basmati rice layered with tender pieces of mutton, cooked with saffron and spices.

Tandoori Specialties:

  • Tandoori Chicken: Half or full chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, grilled in the tandoor.

  • Tandoori Mixed Grill: An assortment of tandoori kebabs, including chicken, lamb, and seafood.

  • Tandoori Roti/Naan: Traditional Indian bread baked in the tandoor, available in plain, butter, garlic, and cheese variations.

Rice and Breads:

  • Steamed Basmati Rice: Fluffy, fragrant long-grain rice.

  • Jeera Rice: Basmati rice cooked with cumin seeds and aromatic spices.

  • Garlic Naan: Tandoor-baked bread topped with garlic and butter.

  • Peshawari Naan: Sweet naan stuffed with a mixture of nuts and dried fruits.

Sides:

  • Raita: Cooling yogurt mixed with cucumber, mint, and spices.

  • Papadum: Crisp lentil wafers served with assorted chutneys.

  • Indian Pickles: Spicy and tangy pickles made from seasonal fruits and vegetables.

Desserts:

  • Gulab Jamun: Deep-fried milk dumplings soaked in a sweet syrup.

  • Rasmalai: Soft paneer patties soaked in sweetened, flavored milk.

  • Mango Kulfi: Traditional Indian ice cream made with mango pulp and garnished with pistachios.

Beverages:

  • Masala Chai: Spiced Indian tea brewed with milk and aromatic spices.

  • Mango Lassi: A refreshing yogurt-based drink blended with mango pulp.

  • Rose Sharbat: A sweet and fragrant rose-flavored drink.

  • Selection of Fine Wines and Spirits:

Chef's Specials:

  • Royal Thali: A grand platter featuring a selection of the day's specialties, including appetizers, main courses, rice, bread, and dessert.

  • Nawabi Biryani: A royal biryani made with your choice of meat or vegetables, cooked with fragrant spices and saffron.

Kids' Menu:

  • Mini Chicken Tikka: Tender chicken pieces marinated and grilled, served with fries.

  • Cheese Naan Pizza: Naan bread topped with cheese and mild tomato sauce.

  • Sweet Lassi: A sweetened yogurt drink, perfect for kids.
