Tandoori Specialties:

  • Tandoori Chicken: Half or full chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, grilled in the tandoor.
  • Tandoori Mixed Grill: An assortment of tandoori kebabs, including chicken, lamb, and seafood.
  • Tandoori Roti/Naan: Traditional Indian bread baked in the tandoor, available in plain, butter, garlic, and cheese variations.
